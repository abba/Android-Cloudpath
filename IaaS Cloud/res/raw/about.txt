Cloud Path is an Infrastructure as service application.

It serves as a one-stop destination for company decision makers 
currently using cloud infrastructure as a service (IaaS), 
thinking of adopting or migrating to cloud.

Cloud Path offers the following features and functionalities:

Educating users on cloud infrastructure as a service (IaaS)
Evaluation of current infrastructure, determine requirements and 
business needs toward cloud adoption or migration.
Recommend and select providers.
Provider simplified infrastructure location such offering location of servers, 
alerts on low performance, hardware and enable turning on and off of hardware.
